Solar Storms

Coronal Mass Ejection

These are millions of tons of super-heated gas shooting off from the surface of the sun and hurtling 90 million miles toward Earth.

When it hits Earth’s magnetic field, it triggers a geomagnetic storm which can cripple us! These storms are caused by 11-year Sun cycles that shift the polarity of the energy source's magnetic field.
