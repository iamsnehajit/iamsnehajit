Welcome to KCJCCSLTD

Kona Chamrail Jagadishpur Co-operative Credit Society Limited (KCJCCSLTD) is situated at village Khalia under PS of Liluah. The post office is Chamrail and the nearest railway station, Kona is about 1.5 km away from the area.We have three branch offices at Kona, Chamrail and Jagadishpur. We earnestly believed the principle of the Cooperative Society "Live and Let Live" and rigorously persued several objectives namely:

  • to identify the need of the villagers and try to solve them;
  • to depict the duties of the members with sincerity, honesty and self reliability;
  • to plan properly and execute with the support of the people and
  • to evaluate the performance on regular basis.INFRASRUCTURE AND PROGRESS
  • to incresing social security through life insurence.
